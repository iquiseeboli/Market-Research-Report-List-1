<p><p>The Automatic Tray Forming Machine market includes two main types: Vertical Form Fill Seal (V-FFS) and Horizontal Form Fill Seal (H-FFS) machines. V-FFS machines operate in a vertical orientation, efficiently forming, filling, and sealing products in flexible packaging. In contrast, H-FFS machines work horizontally, offering better stability and control for heavier products. Both types are essential in packaging applications, enhancing production efficiency and ensuring optimal presentation and protection of goods during storage and transit.</p></p>
