<p><p>The Fresnel lenses market is categorized into two primary types: imaging and non-imaging. Imaging type Fresnel lenses are designed to focus light into a specific image, often used in applications like projectors and cameras where image clarity is crucial. In contrast, non-imaging type Fresnel lenses diffuse or redirect light without forming an image, making them ideal for applications such as solar concentrators and lighting systems where light distribution is more important than image quality.</p></p>
