<p><strong>The Low-Frequency Sound-Absorbing Insulation Material Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Closed-cell Foams</li><li>Open-cell Foams</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Low-frequency sound-absorbing insulation materials primarily come in two types: closed-cell foams and open-cell foams. Closed-cell foams feature tightly packed cells, making them denser and more effective at blocking sound while also providing moisture resistance. These are ideal for environments needing high sound attenuation. Open-cell foams, on the other hand, have interconnected cells that allow airflow, offering excellent sound absorption characteristics for mid- to high-frequency sounds. This makes them suitable for applications where ventilation is important alongside sound dampening.</p></p>
