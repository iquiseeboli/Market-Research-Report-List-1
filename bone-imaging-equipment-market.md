CT scanners offer detailed cross-sectional images for precise evaluation. MRI systems utilize magnetic fields to visualize soft tissue and bone structure. EOS imaging systems provide low-dose 2D and 3D imaging for orthopedic assessments. Ultrasound uses sound waves for real-time imaging, while nuclear imaging systems utilize radioactive tracers to capture bone metabolism and pathology, enhancing diagnostic capabilities.</p></p>
