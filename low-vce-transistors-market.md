<p><strong>The Low VCE Transistors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PNP</li><li>NPN</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The low VCE transistor market primarily consists of two types: PNP and NPN transistors. NPN transistors facilitate current flow from the collector to the emitter when a positive voltage is applied to the base, making them popular in digital and switching applications. PNP transistors, on the other hand, enable current flow from the emitter to the collector when a negative voltage is applied to the base, often used in complementary circuits. Both types are essential for efficient signal amplification and electronic circuit design.</p></p>
