<p><p>The meat speciation testing market includes several key types: PCR (Polymerase Chain Reaction), ELISA (Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay), and molecular diagnostics. PCR is a highly sensitive method that amplifies DNA to detect specific meat species. ELISA utilizes antibodies to identify proteins unique to certain meats, offering a reliable alternative. Molecular diagnostics encompasses various techniques for identifying genetic material, ensuring accurate meat species identification. Together, they address food safety concerns and regulatory compliance in the meat industry.</p></p>
