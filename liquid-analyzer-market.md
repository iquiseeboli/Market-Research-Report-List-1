<p><p>The liquid analyzer market encompasses various technologies for monitoring water quality. Turbidity analyzers measure cloudiness, indicating the presence of suspended particles. pH/ORP analyzers assess acidity and oxidation reduction potential, crucial for chemical balance. Total Organic Carbon (TOC) and Chemical Oxygen Demand (COD) analyzers evaluate organic pollutants. Titro analyzers determine concentration through titration methods. Conductivity meters assess ionic content, while chlorine analyzers monitor disinfection levels. Dissolved oxygen analyzers are vital for assessing aquatic health. Each type serves specific environmental and industrial applications.</p></p>
