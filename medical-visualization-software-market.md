<p><p>The medical visualization software market encompasses various types, primarily including image post-processing systems and image annotation systems. Image post-processing systems enhance and refine medical images, improving their quality for analysis and diagnosis. These systems apply techniques like filtering, contrast adjustment, and 3D rendering. In contrast, image annotation systems are used to label and categorize medical images for research and diagnostic purposes, facilitating the training of algorithms and improving the accuracy of machine learning models in medical applications.</p></p>
