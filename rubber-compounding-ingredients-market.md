<p><p>The rubber compounding ingredients market encompasses several key types. Vulcanizing agents, such as sulfur, enhance rubber's elasticity and durability through cross-linking. Protective agents, including antioxidants and UV stabilizers, prevent degradation. Reinforcing agents, like carbon black and silica, improve strength and resilience. Bonding agents facilitate adhesion between rubber and other materials. Additional components, categorized as "others," may include processing aids and colorants, each contributing to the performance and characteristics of rubber products in various applications.</p></p>
