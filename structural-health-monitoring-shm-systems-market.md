<p><p>Structural Health Monitoring (SHM) systems are categorized into wired and wireless types. Wired SHM systems use physical cables to connect sensors to data acquisition units, offering reliable data transmission and minimal interference but often require extensive installation work. In contrast, wireless SHM systems utilize wireless communication technologies, providing greater flexibility and ease of installation, especially in remote or hard-to-access locations. Both systems aim to ensure the safety and longevity of structures by continuously monitoring their health and identifying potential issues.</p></p>
