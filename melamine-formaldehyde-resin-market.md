<p><p>The Melamine Formaldehyde Resin market is primarily divided into two types: Methylation Melamine Formaldehyde Resin and Unmethylated Melamine Formaldehyde Resin. Methylation Melamine Formaldehyde Resin is produced by modifying melamine with methyl groups, enhancing stability and thermal resistance, making it ideal for coatings and laminates. In contrast, Unmethylated Melamine Formaldehyde Resin, known for its higher binding strength, is commonly used in adhesives and agricultural applications. Each type serves distinct purposes in industries such as construction, automotive, and textiles.</p></p>
