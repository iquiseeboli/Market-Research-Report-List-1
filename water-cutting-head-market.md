<p><strong>The Water Cutting Head Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sand Water Knife</li><li>Pure Water Water Knife</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The water cutting head market comprises two primary types: sand water knives and pure water knives. Sand water knives utilize a mixture of water and abrasive materials, like sand, to cut through tough materials, making them ideal for high-density applications such as metal fabrication. On the other hand, pure water knives rely solely on high-pressure water jets to achieve precision cutting in softer materials, such as foam or food products, offering a cleaner and more eco-friendly option for delicate applications.</p></p>
