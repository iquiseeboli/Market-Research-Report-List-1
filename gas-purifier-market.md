<p><p>The gas purifier market comprises three main types: Single Column Purifiers, Double Column Purifiers, and Multi-Column Purifiers. Single Column Purifiers handle a single gas flow, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness. Double Column Purifiers provide parallel processing, enhancing efficiency and redundancy for increased reliability. Multi-Column Purifiers utilize multiple columns for robust gas treatment, allowing for continuous operation and complex separation processes. Each type serves different operational needs, balancing efficiency, complexity, and pricing based on industrial applications.</p></p>
