<p><strong>The Self-Retaining Retractors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Use Type</li><li>Reusable Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The self-retaining retractors market is segmented into two main types: single-use and reusable. Single-use retractors are designed for one-time use, offering convenience and reducing the risk of cross-contamination in surgical settings. They are often pre-sterilized and can be disposed of after surgery. In contrast, reusable retractors are designed for multiple uses, made from durable materials, and require thorough sterilization between procedures. This type may be more cost-effective over time, appealing to hospitals with higher surgical volumes.</p></p>
