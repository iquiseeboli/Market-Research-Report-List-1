<p><strong>The Screw Fasteners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>External Thread Fasteners</li><li>Internal Threaded Fasteners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The screw fasteners market comprises two main types: external thread fasteners and internal threaded fasteners. External thread fasteners, such as screws and bolts, feature threads on the outside, enabling them to secure components by engaging with pre-drilled holes or nuts. Internal threaded fasteners, including nuts and certain types of screws, possess threads on the inside, allowing them to hold and fasten onto external-threaded screws or bolts. Both types play crucial roles in various applications, ensuring reliable connections in construction and manufacturing.</p></p>
