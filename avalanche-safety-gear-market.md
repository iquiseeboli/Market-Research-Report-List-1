<p><p>The Avalanche Safety Gear market comprises two main types: airbags and security backpacks. Airbags are essential for increasing a skier or snowboarder's buoyancy during an avalanche, helping to keep them on the surface of the snow and reducing the risk of suffocation. Security backpacks, often equipped with features like avalanche probes and shovels, provide added safety by allowing users to carry essential rescue tools. Together, these products enhance safety for winter sports enthusiasts venturing into backcountry areas prone to avalanches.</p></p>
