<p><ul><li>TTL IC (Transistor-transistor Logic)</li><li>CMOS Logic IC (Complementary Metal Oxide Semiconductor)</li><li>BiCMOS Logic IC (Bipolar CMOS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Communication Special Purpose Logic IC market comprises various types of integrated circuits, including TTL, CMOS, and BiCMOS technologies. TTL ICs utilize bipolar transistors for high-speed operation and reliable performance, making them suitable for digital circuits. CMOS ICs combine both n-channel and p-channel transistors, offering low power consumption and high noise immunity, ideal for battery-operated devices. BiCMOS ICs blend the advantages of bipolar and CMOS technologies, delivering high speed and low power, catering to applications requiring high-performance signal processing.</p></p>
