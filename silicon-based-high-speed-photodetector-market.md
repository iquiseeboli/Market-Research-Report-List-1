<p><p>The silicon-based high-speed photodetector market includes Silicon Drift Detectors (SDDs) and Silicon Photomultipliers (SiPMs). SDDs are known for their excellent energy resolution and fast response times, making them ideal for applications in X-ray spectroscopy and particle detection. In contrast, SiPMs offer high sensitivity and photon detection efficiency, making them suitable for low-light applications such as medical imaging and time-of-flight measurements. Both technologies are advancing in performance, expanding their use in scientific and commercial sectors.</p></p>
