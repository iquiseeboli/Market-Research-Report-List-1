<p><ul><li>Embedded</li><li>Tethered</li><li>Integrated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Car sharing telematics encompasses three market types: embedded, tethered, and integrated. Embedded telematics involves built-in systems within vehicles, allowing seamless data collection and management. Tethered telematics connects external devices, like smartphones, to vehicles for data access and control. Integrated telematics combines both embedded and tethered approaches, ensuring comprehensive connectivity and functionality. Each type enhances user experience, fleet management, and operational efficiency, catering to the evolving needs of car sharing businesses and their customers.</p></p>
