<p><p>The smart baby thermometer market includes two primary types: smart baby ear thermometers and smart baby infrared thermometers. Smart baby ear thermometers utilize infrared technology to measure temperature through the eardrum, providing quick and accurate results, ideal for infants. In contrast, smart baby infrared thermometers measure surface temperature without direct contact, making them non-intrusive and suitable for infants who may be fussy. Both types offer connectivity features, enabling parents to track temperatures via apps, enhancing health monitoring for their children.</p></p>
