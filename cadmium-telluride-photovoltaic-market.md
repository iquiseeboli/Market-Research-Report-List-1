Single crystal CdTe offers high efficiency and superior performance but is more costly to produce. Large grain CdTe has a balance between cost and efficiency, making it suitable for commercial applications. Standard cells, often made from polycrystalline materials, are less expensive and widely produced, but they typically exhibit lower efficiencies.
