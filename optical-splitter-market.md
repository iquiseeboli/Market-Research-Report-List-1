<p><strong>The Optical Splitter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fused Biconic Tapered Splitters</li><li>Planar Lightwave Circuit Splitters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical splitter market includes two main types: Fused Biconic Tapered Splitters (FBTs) and Planar Lightwave Circuit (PLC) Splitters. FBTs utilize the fusion and tapering of optical fibers to divide light signals, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness, typically suitable for smaller networks. In contrast, PLC splitters use waveguide technology on a substrate, allowing for more precise splitting ratios and better performance in larger networks. Both types are essential for efficient optical signal distribution in fiber-optic communication systems.</p></p>
