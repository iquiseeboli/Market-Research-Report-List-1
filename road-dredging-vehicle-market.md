<p><p>The road dredging vehicle market can be categorized into two main types: trailed type and self-propelled type. Trailed type vehicles rely on another machine for movement, making them flexible and cost-effective for various applications. Both types serve distinct needs in the construction and maintenance of roadways, influencing market dynamics based on efficiency, ease of use, and operational costs.</p></p>
