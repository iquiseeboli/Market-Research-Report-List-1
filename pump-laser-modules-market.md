<p><ul><li>Single Mode Pump Lasers</li><li>Multi-Mode Pump Lasers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pump laser modules market consists of two primary types: single mode pump lasers and multi-mode pump lasers. Single mode pump lasers offer a single spatial mode, providing high power and precision, making them ideal for applications requiring focused light, such as fiber amplifiers. Conversely, multi-mode pump lasers support multiple spatial modes, enabling higher output power and broader efficiency, suitable for applications like industrial and medical lasers where beam quality is less critical but high energy delivery is essential.</p></p>
