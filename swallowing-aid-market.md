<p><p>The swallowing aid market encompasses three primary types: gel, liquid, and spray formulations. Liquid aids provide a smooth, easy-to-swallow option, often enhanced with flavors for better palatability. Spray options deliver concentrated formulations directly into the mouth, promoting easier swallowing through quick and effective hydration.
