<p><p>The panel displays market is primarily divided into two main types: Organic Light Emitting Diode (OLED) and Liquid Crystal Display (LCD). OLED displays utilize organic compounds that emit light when an electric current passes through, offering superior color accuracy, contrast, and flexibility. In contrast, LCDs use liquid crystals illuminated by backlight, providing a more cost-effective solution with good image quality but generally lower contrast ratios. Both technologies cater to varying consumer needs, with OLED appealing to high-end markets and LCD remaining popular for mainstream applications.</p></p>
