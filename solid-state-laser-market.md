<p><strong>The Solid-state Laser Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous Wave Solid-State Laser</li><li>Pulsed Solid State Laser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solid-state laser market comprises two main types: Continuous Wave (CW) solid-state lasers and pulsed solid-state lasers. CW solid-state lasers emit a constant, uninterrupted beam of light, making them ideal for applications requiring consistent energy output, such as in cutting and welding. In contrast, pulsed solid-state lasers produce high-energy bursts of light over short durations, allowing for precision tasks like drilling and material processing. Both types serve diverse industries, including manufacturing, medical, and telecommunications, enhancing performance and efficiency.</p></p>
