<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Light Vehicle Steering Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electric Powered Hydraulic Steering</li><li>Electric Powered Steering Systems</li><li>Hydraulic Powered Steering</li><li>Manual Steering</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The light vehicle steering market comprises four main types: </p><p>1. **Electric Powered Hydraulic Steering (EPHS)** combines hydraulic assistance with electric control for enhanced responsiveness and fuel efficiency. </p><p>2. **Electric Powered Steering Systems (EPS)** utilize electric motors for steering, offering flexibility, light weight, and reduced energy consumption. </p><p>3. **Hydraulic Powered Steering (HPS)** relies on hydraulic fluid for steering assistance, providing a traditional feel and greater feedback but is less energy efficient. </p><p>4. **Manual Steering** uses a mechanical linkage, requiring driver effort without assistance, often found in older or simpler vehicle models. Each type serves varying performance, efficiency, and cost needs in modern automotive design.</p></p>
