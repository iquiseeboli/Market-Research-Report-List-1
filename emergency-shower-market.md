<p><strong>The Emergency Shower Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Freestanding</li><li>Wall Mounted</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The emergency shower market includes two primary types: freestanding and wall-mounted showers. Freestanding emergency showers are self-contained units that can be easily positioned in various locations, providing flexibility in accessibility. They are often used in areas where plumbing isn't readily available. Wall-mounted emergency showers, on the other hand, are fixed to walls and typically require direct plumbing connections, making them ideal for spaces with pre-existing infrastructure. Both types are crucial for ensuring safety in hazardous environments, promoting quick decontamination.</p></p>
