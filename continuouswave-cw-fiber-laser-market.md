<p><p>The Continuous Wave (CW) Fiber Laser market is divided into two main types: Single Mode and Multi Mode. Single Mode fiber lasers provide a narrow beam with high coherence, ideal for applications requiring precision, such as medical procedures and telecommunications. Multi Mode fiber lasers, on the other hand, have a larger core diameter, allowing multiple light modes to propagate, which enhances power delivery and intensity, making them suitable for industrial cutting and welding applications. Both types cater to distinct operational needs.</p></p>
