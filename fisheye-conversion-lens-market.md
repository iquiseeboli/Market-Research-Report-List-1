Full-frame fisheye lenses capture a wide 180-degree perspective, producing a hemispherical image that utilizes the entire sensor area. Circular fisheye lenses also provide a 180-degree view but produce a circular image within the frame, leaving black corners. Other fisheye types may include specific formats or unique designs, offering versatility for creative expression in photography, videography, and special effects.</p></p>
