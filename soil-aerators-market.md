<p><ul><li>Mechanical</li><li>Pneumatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The soil aerators market consists of two primary types: mechanical and pneumatic. Mechanical aerators utilize physical tools, such as spikes or blades, to break up soil and enhance air exchange, promoting root growth and nutrient uptake. Pneumatic aerators, on the other hand, use compressed air to create voids in the soil, improving aeration and drainage without soil disturbance. Both types serve essential functions in agriculture and landscaping, supporting healthier soil ecosystems and optimizing plant performance.</p></p>
