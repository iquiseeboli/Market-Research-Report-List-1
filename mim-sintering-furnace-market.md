<p><p>The MIM (Metal Injection Molding) sintering furnace market is primarily divided into two types: batch furnaces and continuous furnaces. Batch furnaces process multiple loads simultaneously, allowing for flexibility and precision in managing small production runs. In contrast, continuous furnaces operate in an uninterrupted flow, providing efficient and high-volume production, ideal for large-scale manufacturing. Both types cater to different production needs, emphasizing efficiency, temperature control, and atmosphere management to ensure optimal sintering of MIM components.</p></p>
