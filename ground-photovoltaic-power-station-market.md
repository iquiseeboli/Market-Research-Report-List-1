<p><p>The Ground Photovoltaic Power Station market comprises two primary types: Crystalline Silicon Photovoltaic Power Stations and Thin Film Photovoltaic Power Stations. In contrast, Thin Film Photovoltaic Power Stations employ layers of semiconductor materials, offering flexibility and lightweight characteristics but generally lower efficiency.
