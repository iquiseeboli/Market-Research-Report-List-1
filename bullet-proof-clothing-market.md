<p><p>The bulletproof clothing market is primarily divided into hard vests and soft vests. Hard vests incorporate rigid ballistic plates made from materials like ceramic or polyethylene, offering high levels of protection against high-caliber rounds, making them suitable for law enforcement and military use. Soft vests, on the other hand, are made from flexible materials such as Kevlar, providing comfort and mobility while protecting against lower-caliber threats. Each type serves distinct needs, balancing protection, comfort, and weight considerations.</p></p>
