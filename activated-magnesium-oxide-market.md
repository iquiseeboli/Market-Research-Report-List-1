<p><p>Activated magnesium oxide is categorized into three market types based on its activity levels: high activity, moderately active, and low activity. High activity magnesium oxide is used in applications requiring superior chemical reactivity, such as in pharmaceuticals and environmental applications. Moderately active magnesium oxide finds use in industries like rubber and plastics, offering balanced performance. Low activity magnesium oxide primarily serves as a filler in construction and ceramics, where less reactivity is sufficient for effective performance.</p></p>
