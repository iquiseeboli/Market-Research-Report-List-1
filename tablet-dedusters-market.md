<p><ul><li>Spiral Path Deduster</li><li>Elevating Path Deduster</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tablet dedusters are essential machines in the pharmaceutical industry, designed to remove dust and debris from tablets after the compression process. The market can be categorized into two primary types: Spiral Path Dedusters and Elevating Path Dedusters. Spiral Path Dedusters utilize a helical design to guide tablets through a cleaning chamber, effectively removing unwanted particles. In contrast, Elevating Path Dedusters employ an upward movement mechanism, enhancing the efficiency of dust removal while ensuring minimal tablet breakage during the process.</p></p>
