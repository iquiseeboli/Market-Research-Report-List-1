<p><p>Grain Oriented Electrical Steel (GOES) is categorized into three main market types: High Magnetic Strength, Conventional, and Domain Refinement. High Magnetic Strength GOES offers superior performance and energy efficiency, making it ideal for advanced applications in transformers and generators. Conventional GOES is widely used for standard electrical applications due to its cost-effectiveness. Domain Refinement GOES features enhanced magnetic properties through controlled grain structures, optimizing performance for specialized high-efficiency devices. Each type caters to specific industry needs, driving innovation in electrical steel applications.</p></p>
