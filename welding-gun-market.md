<p><strong>The Welding Gun Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Arc Welding</li><li>Resistance welding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The welding gun market is primarily divided into two types: arc welding and resistance welding. Arc welding guns utilize an electric arc to generate heat, melting the base and filler materials to create a strong bond, commonly used in construction and manufacturing. In contrast, resistance welding guns apply heat generated by electric resistance between workpieces, efficiently joining metals through pressure, and are widely used in automotive and light manufacturing. Both methods play crucial roles in various industrial applications.</p></p>
