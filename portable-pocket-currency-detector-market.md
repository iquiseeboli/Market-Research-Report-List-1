<p><p>The Portable Pocket Currency Detector market encompasses various detection methods, primarily fluorescence detection and magnetic detection. Fluorescence detection utilizes ultraviolet light to identify security features in currency that fluoresce, distinguishing genuine notes from counterfeits. In contrast, magnetic detection relies on the magnetic properties embedded in authentic currency, which can be sensed by the detector. Both methods aim to enhance the accuracy and efficiency of authenticating banknotes, catering to users such as retailers, collectors, and law enforcement agencies.</p></p>
