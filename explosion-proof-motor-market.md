<p><p>The explosion-proof motor market is categorized primarily into EXd Type and Increased-Safety Type, alongside other variants. EXd Type motors are designed to contain explosive gases within the housing, preventing ignition. Increased-Safety Type motors incorporate features to reduce the risk of ignition from electrical components without relying solely on containment. Other types may include encapsulated or purged motors, tailored for specific environments and safety needs. Each type serves to enhance operational safety in hazardous locations.</p></p>
