<p><p>The glass stemware market is divided primarily into two types: crystal glass and non-crystal glass. Crystal glass is known for its high clarity, brilliance, and resonance, often enhanced with lead oxide or other materials, making it popular for upscale dining and special occasions. Non-crystal glass, typically made from soda-lime or tempered glass, is more durable and cost-effective, appealing for everyday use. Both types cater to different consumer preferences, balancing aesthetics and practicality in various settings.</p></p>
