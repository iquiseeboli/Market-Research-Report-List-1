<p><strong>The Stationary Barcode Scanner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Laser Scanner</li><li>Linear Imager</li><li>2D Imager Scanner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stationary barcode scanner market comprises three main types: laser scanners, linear imagers, and 2D imagers. Laser scanners use laser beams to read barcodes quickly and accurately, ideal for high-volume environments. Linear imagers capture images of barcodes using a single line of sensors and are effective for reading conventional barcodes. 2D imagers, on the other hand, can decode both one-dimensional and two-dimensional barcodes, offering versatility for varied applications, including scanning mobile barcodes and QR codes.</p></p>
