<p><ul><li>Upper Extremity Prosthetics</li><li>Lower Extremity Prosthetics</li><li>Liners</li><li>Sockets</li><li>Modular Components</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The orthopedic prosthetic devices market encompasses various products designed to restore mobility and function. Upper extremity prosthetics focus on artificial arms and hands, improving grip and control. Lower extremity prosthetics include artificial legs and feet, enhancing walking and stability. Liners provide cushioning and comfort between the residual limb and prosthetic socket. Sockets are the interface that connects the prosthetic device to the limb. Modular components include interchangeable parts, allowing for customized fittings and adjustments to better meet individual needs.</p></p>
