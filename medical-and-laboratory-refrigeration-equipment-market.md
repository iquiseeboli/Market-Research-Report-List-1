<p><p>The medical and laboratory refrigeration equipment market is categorized into low temperature refrigeration and ultra-low temperature refrigeration. Low temperature refrigeration equipment operates typically between -10°C to -40°C, ideal for storing vaccines, biological samples, and medications. Ultra-low temperature refrigeration equipment functions at temperatures below -40°C, crucial for preserving materials like pharmaceuticals, plasma, and sensitive biological specimens that require stringent environmental controls. Both types ensure the integrity and efficacy of medical and laboratory supplies by providing reliable temperature management.</p></p>
