<p><p>The robotic camera market is primarily categorized into screw type and manipulator type cameras. Screw type cameras utilize a rotating mechanism for capturing images, offering precise control and stability, making them ideal for applications like industrial inspections and surveillance. In contrast, manipulator type cameras employ robotic arms to achieve dynamic movement and versatility, suitable for live event broadcasting and film production. Both types enhance operational efficiency and expand creative possibilities in various imaging applications.</p></p>
