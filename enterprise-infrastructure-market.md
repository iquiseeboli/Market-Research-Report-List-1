<p><p>Enterprise infrastructure markets are categorized into three types: public, private, and hybrid. Public infrastructure involves shared resources offered by third-party providers over the internet, suitable for scalability and cost-efficiency. Private infrastructure is dedicated to a single organization, ensuring enhanced security and control over resources. Hybrid infrastructure combines both public and private elements, allowing businesses to leverage the advantages of both while maintaining flexibility and compliance. This approach enables optimal resource allocation tailored to specific operational needs.</p></p>
